PlayAmigos

apps for learning, productivity & fun

PlayAmigos originally started a long time ago as a games publisher name—born back when my friends and I used to build games together. While those early projects have evolved, I wanted the name and that cooperative spirit to stick around.

Today, this hub serves as a curated collection of useful web apps crafted around learning, productivity, and fun games. Each app is designed to be simple, interactive, and genuinely helpful.

New apps are added regularly. You can check out the blog to stay updated on new releases.

Stick around, have fun!
